FoodShare

At FoodShare, we believe that no meal should go to waste while people go hungry. Our platform bridges the gap between generous donors, charitable organizations, and individuals in need, creating a seamless, community-driven food-sharing network. By redistributing surplus food, we’re not just fighting hunger—we’re building a sustainable future, reducing waste, and strengthening connections. Join us in making every meal count! 🍽️♻️

How FoodShare Works 🍽️♻️
🔹 Donors: Easily list surplus food with details like quantity, expiry, and pickup location.
🔹 Charities & Individuals: Browse available food, claim what you need, and arrange pickup.
🔹 Instant Alerts: Get real-time notifications when fresh food is up for grabs.
🔹 Seamless Pickup: Collect your food, confirm the handover, and help reduce waste!
💡 Every meal saved is a life nourished—let’s share the goodness!
